introduction

helping education for v i This is a very small company which has created this platform related to reading or studies for visually impaired people.

Audiobooks are provided for visually impaired persons on this platform

This platform has been created so that if a blind person does not know how to read Braille, then he can listen to or download the audio of all the subjects of his own class.

Although this platform is made for visually impaired people, but the normal person who is able to see live can also use this platform for studies.

The only and only purpose of making this platform is that those who are visually impaired and those who are normal are unable to complete their studies for some reason, then they can pursue their studies by listening to audio on this platform.

We hope that whoever comes to this platform once, he will definitely learn something or the other and go away from here.

Thank you very much for visiting this platform, we hope that you will keep reading like this and keep moving forward.
